## Short Description

Agent2Research is a medical research AI tool intelligence and decision guide for literature search, Zotero workflows, systematic reviews, academic writing, statistics, bioinformatics, and medical imaging.

## Who It Helps

- Medical researchers
- Clinicians doing research
- Graduate students
- Systematic review teams
- Bioinformatics researchers
- Medical imaging researchers
- Research teams evaluating AI tools
