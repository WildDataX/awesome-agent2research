## What Is Agent2Research?

[Agent2Research](https://agent2research.com/) is a medical research AI tool intelligence hub. It helps researchers choose tools by research task, workflow fit, limitations, and evidence quality instead of browsing a generic list of AI products.

The site is especially useful when a researcher is asking:

- Which AI tool should I try first for this research task?
- Is this tool useful for literature search, Zotero, systematic reviews, writing, statistics, bioinformatics, or medical imaging?
- What are the practical limitations before I put it into a real research workflow?
- How do I compare tools without being misled by marketing claims?

## What The Tool Library Covers

Agent2Research currently covers common medical research tool categories, including:

- Literature search and evidence discovery
- Zotero workflows and reference management
- Paper reading, PDF translation, and research note-taking
- Systematic review screening and evidence tables
- Academic writing and manuscript revision
- Scientific visualization and research figures
- Statistics tools such as JASP, jamovi, and R ecosystem packages
- Bioinformatics, single-cell, multi-omics, and medical NLP
- Medical imaging, segmentation, reconstruction, and modeling tools
- Open-source projects and research automation workflows

## Editorial Positioning

Agent2Research is not a raw directory. Each useful entry should help the reader answer three practical questions:

1. What task is this tool good for?
2. Who should try it first, and who should avoid it?
3. What must still be checked manually before using the output in research?

This makes the site more suitable for researchers, clinicians, graduate students, systematic review teams, medical AI builders, librarians, and research operations teams who care about workflow reliability.
